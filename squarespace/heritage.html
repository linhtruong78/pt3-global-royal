The Origin

Born from the Heart
of Ancient Persia

For thousands of years, the fertile lands of Persia have been celebrated for producing some of the world's most extraordinary grapes. Persian royalty understood that wine was not merely a beverage — it was an expression of civilization, culture, and refinement.

PT3 Global Royal was founded to honor this magnificent legacy — to bottle the spirit of Persian royal heritage and present it to the modern world as a collectible, luxury experience worthy of the tradition it represents.

"Every bottle carries the memory of a civilization that made beauty a way of life."

Our Journey

The Royal Milestones

Ancient Persia
The Royal Vine
Persian royalty cultivated the finest grapevines in the fertile highlands, developing winemaking traditions that would influence cultures across the ancient world for millennia.
Heritage Era
Craft & Ceremony
Wine became central to Persian royal ceremony and hospitality — a symbol of abundance, refinement, and the sophisticated culture of one of history's greatest civilizations.
Modern Vision
PT3 Global Royal Founded
Inspired by this magnificent heritage, PT3 Global Royal was established to bring the spirit of Persian royal tradition into the modern luxury beverage world.
Reserve Collection
Arak Sagi Shiraz Reserve
The flagship reserve wine collection was launched — premium Shiraz crafted with uncompromising standards, aged to perfection, and presented in collectible luxury packaging.
Premium Beer
Shams Beer — Light of Luxury
The Shams premium beer collection extended the brand's vision — bringing the same commitment to elegance, craftsmanship, and heritage to the world of premium beer.
Global Expansion
International Royal Presence
PT3 Global Royal expanded into luxury markets worldwide — establishing partnerships with premier hospitality brands, exclusive retailers, and international distributors.
