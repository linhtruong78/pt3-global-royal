The Story of Shams

Named for the Persian Sun

In ancient Persian culture, the sun — Shams — was a symbol of power, warmth, and the life-giving force that sustained civilization. Persian kings revered it. Artists celebrated it. Poets wrote of its eternal brilliance.

Shams Premium Beer carries this legacy — a beer that shines with the same warmth, brilliance, and cultural pride. It is a premium product for a premium moment, crafted for those who understand that luxury is not a price point, but a standard of living.

Each can and bottle is designed to catch the light — gold details that glow, elegant silhouettes that stand apart, and a taste that lives up to its name.
